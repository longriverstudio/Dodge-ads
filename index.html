Privacy Policy for Dodge

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Dodge is a mobile arcade game developed by Long River Studio.

2. Data Collection

Dodge does not collect or store personal data such as names, email addresses, or account information.

The game stores certain data locally on your device, including:

  • Game settings
  • High scores

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

3. Advertising

Dodge uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Basic device information
  • Interaction data related to advertisements

This data may be used for ad delivery, personalization, and performance measurement.

All data transmitted by third-party advertising services is encrypted in transit.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:

Google Privacy Policy

4. User Rights (EU/EEA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have rights under GDPR. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device settings.
