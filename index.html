Privacy Policy for Dodge Chaos

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Dodge Chaos is a mobile arcade game developed by Long River Studio.

2. Data Collection

The game itself does not collect personal information directly.

However, the game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Interaction data with ads

3. Advertising

The game uses Google AdMob to serve ads. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user consent and region.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:

Google Privacy Policy

4. User Rights (EU/EEA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have rights under GDPR. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device settings.
